# Hero Hierarchy

The homepage hero needs to answer three questions immediately:

1. Who is this?
2. What kind of engineer is he?
3. Where should a recruiter click first?

## Current hierarchy targets

- Name is the dominant element.
- The one-line positioning statement comes next.
- Email, LinkedIn, and Resume are the clearest outbound actions.
- GitHub is present, but visually lower priority than recruiter-first actions.

## Anti-clutter rules

- Avoid long defensive taglines.
- Avoid repeating school, location, and status in multiple places.
- Avoid turning the right-side profile card into a stats dump.

## Visual rules

- The portrait should do trust-building work.
- Buttons should be easy to read in one pass.
- The scroll cue should feel calm, not loud.
