# KalaAI

KalaAI is framed as a consumer-facing AI marketplace rather than an abstract computer vision demo.

## Core message

- Shoppers discover artisan products through a feed and marketplace flow.
- AI helps identify likely cultural origin from product imagery.
- Discovery and AI both live inside the same shopping experience.

## Homepage proof points

- `120+` cultural detection categories
- `5` product surfaces
- AI origin detection from imagery
- Live deployment on Vercel

## Product surfaces

- Home
- Shorts Feed
- Marketplace
- Trends
- Account

## Visual direction

- The window should feel like an active marketplace state.
- Discovery, listings, and AI output should all be visible at a glance.
- Avoid making the AI feel like a detached lab tool.
